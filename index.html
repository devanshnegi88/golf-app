How It Works

Four steps. Endless impact.

💳

Subscribe

Choose monthly (£9.99) or yearly (£89.99). Your fee fuels the prize pool and funds your chosen charity — every month, automatically.

⛳

Log Scores

Enter your last 5 Stableford scores (1–45). Your numbers become your draw tickets — adding a 6th removes the oldest automatically.

🎯

Monthly Draw

Admin runs a 5-number draw each month using our random or algorithmic engine. Match 3, 4, or all 5 of your scores to win.

🏆

Win & Give

Prize split equally among all matching players. Jackpot rolls over until claimed. Your charity gets paid regardless — every single month.
