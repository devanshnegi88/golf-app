How Par4Good Works

Everything you need to know — from subscribing to collecting your winnings and choosing your charity.

1

Subscribe to the platform

Choose either a Monthly plan (£9.99/month) or a Yearly plan (£89.99/year) — saving 25%. Your subscription is processed securely via Stripe and renews automatically.

Your subscription unlocks all platform features: score entry, draw participation, and your charity contribution. Non-subscribers can browse charities and view draw results but cannot enter draws or log scores.

50% to prize pool Min. 10% to charity Cancel anytime
2

Choose your charity

During signup (or anytime from your dashboard) you select which charity receives your monthly contribution. You can set any percentage from 10% up to 100% of your subscription fee.

You can also make an independent one-time donation to any listed charity at any time — no subscription required.

Charities list upcoming golf events and you can discover featured causes on the homepage.

3

Enter your Stableford scores

From your dashboard, log your golf scores in Stableford format. Every score must be between 1 and 45, and you must record the date played. Only one score per date is allowed.

The platform retains your most recent 5 scores only. When you add a 6th score, the oldest is automatically removed. Your 5 current scores are displayed in reverse chronological order.

34
28
31
22
36
4

The monthly draw

Each month, an administrator generates a draw of 5 numbers between 1 and 45. The draw can be:

  • RandomStandard lottery-style — completely random 5 numbers
  • AlgorithmicWeighted toward most or least frequent subscriber scores

Before publishing, admin can run a simulation to preview how many winners each tier would produce. Once published, results are final.

With your scores of 34, 28, 31, 22, 36 — you match 22, 28, 31 = 3-number match!

5

Prize pool & distribution

50% of all subscription revenue goes into the monthly prize pool. Here's how it's divided:

5-Number Match
Match all 5 draw numbers to your scores
40% of pool
Jackpot — rolls over if no winner
4-Number Match
Match exactly 4 draw numbers to your scores
35% of pool
Split equally among winners
3-Number Match
Match exactly 3 draw numbers to your scores
25% of pool
Split equally among winners

If multiple players win the same tier, the prize is split equally. If no one matches all 5 numbers, the jackpot rolls over and is added to next month's pool.

6

Claiming your prize

When draw results are published, all matching players are notified. To claim your prize, you must upload a screenshot of your scores from your golf club's platform as proof of play.

An administrator reviews and approves your submission. Once verified, your payout is processed and you'll receive confirmation. The payment status moves through: Pending → Verified → Paid.

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I don't have 5 scores yet?+
You can still enter draws with fewer than 5 scores. Your numbers are simply the scores you currently have logged. However, having all 5 scores gives you the best chance of matching draw numbers.
Can I change my charity after signing up?+
Yes — you can change your chosen charity at any time from the 'My Charity' section of your dashboard. The new selection takes effect from your next billing cycle.
What if I enter the same score on two different dates?+
That's completely fine — the same score value can appear on different dates. What's not allowed is two entries for the same date. Each date can only have one score entry.
How is the jackpot rollover calculated?+
If no player matches all 5 draw numbers in a given month, the jackpot portion (40% of that month's pool) is carried forward and added to next month's jackpot. This continues until someone wins.
How do I cancel my subscription?+
You can cancel anytime from your Dashboard → Subscription → Manage Billing. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you retain access until then.
Is my payment information secure?+
Yes. All payments are processed by Stripe, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor. Par4Good never stores your card details — they're handled entirely by Stripe's secure infrastructure.
